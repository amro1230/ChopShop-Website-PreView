ChopShop Nascar Racing League on iRacing is a competitive and exciting virtual racing league that offers a realistic and thrilling racing experience. With a wide variety of cars, tracks, and events, ChopShop provides the perfect platform for drivers of all skill levels to come together and test their skills against the best in the world.
With dedicated servers and a full race calendar, ChopShop is the perfect place to take your racing career to the next level. With an emphasis on friendly competition and camaraderie, ChopShop is the perfect place for racers to have a great time and challenge themselves. Whether you're a veteran or just starting out, ChopShop is the perfect place to find your passion for racing. The league is broadcasted via Signature E-sports, and we have our own in-house race control, Painter, and strict rule book. There is no IRating caps or minimum, but we ask you race others with respect and above all have fun.
